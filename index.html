SINGAPORE TURF CLUB TO RESUME LOCAL RACING BEHIND CLOSED DOORS
With Singapore moving into Phase Two of the post-circuit breaker period on 19 June 2020, the Club has received approval from the Government to resume racing behind closed doors on 11 July 2020, with strict health and Safe Management Measures in place. Due to the current situation, the Club will announce racing fixtures for July to September 2020 on its racing portal.

To minimise the risks of COVID-19 transmission, the Singapore Racecourse will not re-open to the public for the time being. We thank our customers for their continued support and seek their understanding that these arrangements are necessary to ensure their safety and well-being, in alignment with the Government’s regulations and guidelines.
We are open!
Singapore Turf Club is delighted to welcome all spectators back to the Club for live horse-racing action from 30 April 2022.

The safety and well-being of everyone at the races remain our priority. All patrons must be fully-vaccinated and masks must be worn at all times. Validation of vaccination status and SafeEntry check-in are required and via TraceTogether token or app only.

Please click here for ticketing information.

We thank everyone for your patience, understanding and support and we look forward to welcoming you back to the Club.
The Racing Portal is scheduled for maintenance on 24 Jun 2020 from 1900hrs to 2300hrs (GMT+8). Users may experience intermittent or slow access when browsing the Racing Portal during this period of maintenance.
